What is "Sendit"?

Sendit is a web and mobile app designed for product delivery, clothes, food, flowers, furniture, you name it! It provides real-time tracking of the location of your product, so you can feel safe that your product is on the way.

But how can I use it?

Are you too comfortable in your house and don't want to get out to return what your friend lent you? With Sendit, you can ask for someone to pick up your item and deliver it to your friend. Piece of cake, right?

Or do you want to give other people a hand? You can also be the one making the delivery!

With Sendit you can be one, or both. It's your choice.
